Dolphins!

Facts about Dolphins:

  • Scientific name: Odontoceti

  • Dolphins are small-toothed cetaceans easily recognizable by their curved mouths, which give them a permanent “smile.”

  • The orca (killer whale) is the largest dolphin. Hector's dolphin and Franciscana are two of the smallest.

  • Lifespan varies from around 20 years in the smaller dolphin species to 80 years or more for larger dolphins such as orcas.

  • Called “unihemispheric slow-wave sleep,” dolphins sleep with only half of their brain.

  • They have hair that can be found at the top of the rostrum, and dolphins cannot grow their hair anymore after there childhood.
