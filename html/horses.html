Horses

  • There are over 600 breeds of horses!
  • Horses can have an average lifespan of 30 years.
  • Horses can run as fast as 45 mph.
  • Horses are not native to North America.
  • They were domesticated in Eurasia about 6000 years ago.
  • The most popular breed is the American Quarter Horse with more than 2.8 million horses in the world today.
  • The Sorraia breed is the rarest horse breed with less than 200 left.
  • Horses can move their eyes separately and can see 350 degrees around.
