Wolves:

  • Wolves are the largest member of the Canidae family
  • From nose to tip of tail they measure about 5 - 6.5 ft with males and 4.5 - 6bft with females

  • . Pups are born dead and blind as they are covered in holes or patches within their " caves" to protect them from dangers of being eaten
  • A pack of wolves can range from 2 - 30 wolves as they can run up to 36-38 MPH. There can be differnet types of wolves from grey, black, tan, and white wolves
  • Wolves prey on elk, deer, moose, and other animals. When they hunt they hear up to 6 miles away within a forest
  • Their Jaw can crush nearly 15000 pounds as they can also eat up to 20 pounds of mat in one meal
  • They can swim up to 8 miles and can howl for their pack 10km away
