Butterflies:

  • There are almost 20,000 species of butterflies.
  • The most common butterfly in the US is the Cabbage White.
  • The Giant Swallowtail Butterfly is one of the largest butterflies with a wingspread between 4 to 7 inches.
  • Butterflies have four wings that are actually transparent.
  • The colors seen on butterfly wings are actually miniature scales that reflect light.
  • Butterflies, like other insects, have 6 legs. The first pair helps butterflies taste and smell, the second pair also have sensory receptors, and the third pair help them walk.
  • The average lifetime of a butterfly is one month but can be shorter or longer depending on the species, size, climate and predation.
