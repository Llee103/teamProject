Elephants

  • Elephants are the world’s largest land animal averaging about 3m high and weighing up to 6 tons.
  • There are 2 species of elephant: African and Asian.
  • African elephants have larger ears than Asian elephants.
  • Elephant trunks can pick up things, smell, blow, suck up water, and breath.
  • Elephant tusks are actually teeth that help with feeding or defense.
  • Around 90% of African elephants have been wiped out in the past century because of the ivory trade.
  • Asian elephants populations are also declining as well due to habitat changes.
