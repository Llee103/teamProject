Whales

  • Whales are some of the largest mammals on the planet!
  • The blue whale is the largest animal ever and can grow up to 98ft long (29.9m). Newborn blue whales are about 23 feet (7m) long!
  • There are 41 species of whale.
  • Some whales have teeth to eat large fish, squid and octopus.
  • Other types of whales have baleen plates that sift food - plankton, krill, and small fish- into their mouths. Even though they have teeth, whales do no chew their food.
  • Whales are also known to have whale songs: a long, patterned sequence of sounds.
  • Bowhead whales have over 184 distinct songs rivaling a few species of songbirds! Some humpback whale songs have been featured in album songs.
